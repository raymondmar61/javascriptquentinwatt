while(condition){statements and iteration} Use when you don't know how many times to run a loop.

do{statements} while(condition). Do While loop runs at least once because the condition check is after the do{statements} even if the condition is false. Use when you don't know how many times to run a loop. You do know the loop must run at least once.

for(initialize;condition;increment){statements} Use a for loop when you know how many times you know to loop.
